How To Add a New product to your catalogue

  • Initialize a conversation - enter "/start"
  • Click on the Add new products button and observer the instructions from the response.
  • The instructions says to upload an image, and then add the details of the product as a CAPTION
  • So to do this click on the upload icon in the chat, and pick the product's photo
  • You'll be prompted to add a caption, add the name, followed by the description, followed by the price - in that order
  • For example: Nike Air, Original Nike air, 24500 (Price shouldn't have a comma)
  • 24,500 is a wrong input, also make sure to separate each by commas so you don't get any issues.
